Solution:

We are given that the freelance writer earned $45 on each of the first two days and $75 on each of the next three days. We need to calculate the average daily earnings for these five days.

Step 1: Calculate the total earnings

The earnings for the first two days are:

\[ 2 \times 45 = 90 \]

The earnings for the next three days are:

\[ 3 \times 75 = 225 \]

So, the total earnings over the five days are:

\[ 90 + 225 = 315 \]

Step 2: Calculate the average daily earnings

To find the average, divide the total earnings by the number of days:

\[ \frac{315}{5} = 63 \]

Final Answer:

The average daily earnings for these five days is (E) $63.
